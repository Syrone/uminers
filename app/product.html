The AIRCORE MAX North Edition is an industrial air-cooled container built for high-density operation in harsh, cold climates.
Based on a 40ft High Cube platform, it ensures structural strength, predictable cooling, and long-term reliability.

It operates in ambient temperatures from -40°C to +35°C, maintaining stable performance.
Optimized airflow delivers high efficiency, with a PUE of approximately 1.01.

Designed for a service life of over 10 years, it is CE and CCC certified and offers up to 99.7% availability. Low MTTR and standardized components simplify maintenance and minimize downtime.

High-Density Modular ASIC Hall

The equipment hall is a modular, high-density space for ASIC miners. Its layout of four rows with six shelves each supports up to 288 devices while ensuring clear maintenance access and airflow.

Hardware-Agnostic Heavy-Duty Racks

The racks are hardware-agnostic, compatible with Antminer, Whatsminer, and other major ASIC models without modification. Each shelf supports up to 300 kg and is built with galvanized steel for durability.

Controlled Thermal Architecture

Thermal management uses a hot aisle/cold aisle configuration for controlled airflow. The hall features a rubberized floor, service outlets, and industrial LED lighting, including emergency fixtures for safety.

The container uses a two-tier network architecture for reliable connectivity, traffic aggregation, and centralized equipment management. The design ensures stable, scalable operation with straightforward deployment and maintenance.

Network core: One H3C S5024PV5‑EI aggregation switch provides high‑capacity Layer 2 switching and SFP uplinks. Access layer: Fourteen H3C 1024F switches deliver 24 × 1G RJ45 ports each for high‑density device connections.

Standard data‑center features are supported: VLANs, QoS, IPv6, multicast, and security (DHCP Snooping, ARP Protection). Management interfaces include Web UI, CLI, SNMP, and SSH for centralized control, synchronization, and logging.
